Bar Social Games MVP

I've reviewed the ideation primer. The primer outlines three core game mechanics. To start the MVP spec, we need to decide which one to focus on as the wedge for our first 10 bars.

🎰

Drink Roulette (Intra-table)

A full-screen spin wheel animates on everyone's phone at a table. One person gets charged for the round via Apple Pay.

Viral acquisition • Repeat rounds

💌

Anonymous Buy (Inter-table)

Send a drink across the room to another table with a message or IG handle. Receiver gets a notification.

Cross-table network effect • Social graph

🍺

Surge Game (Bar-wide)

Live progress bar. If the bar hits a target (e.g., 100 pints), everyone gets a flash discount.

B2B retention hook • Inventory management
